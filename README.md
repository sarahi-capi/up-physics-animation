## The Concept ✨
The goal of this project was to test my foundational animation skills and understanding of Blender's physics engine. I choreographed a bouncing ball to perfectly match the tempo and emotional beats of "Married Life," surrounded by 3D recreations of significant objects from the film. 

## Technical Breakdown 🛠️
While the aesthetic is soft and nostalgic, the execution required precise timeline management and spatial logic:

* I managed keyframe interpolation and physics simulations to create a realistic bounce that feels heavy yet bouncy, mapping visual weight to the audio track.
* I modeled and textured stylized assets (recreations from the movie) using optimized geometry within Blender.
* The animation timeline was aligned tightly with the audio waveform to ensure the visual impacts hit exactly on the musical cues.
* Setup a soft, cinematic lighting and background rig to capture the emotional tone of the movie.
* I utilized the Affinity Suite for creating custom textures.

This project, in combination with [A Christmas Bakery](https://github.com/sarahi-capi/the-christmas-bakery-3d) project, led to my successful admission into the **Augmented and Virtual Reality Design** program at **Hochschule Darmstadt**.

## Folder Structure 📁
* `/Renders`: The final rendered video and assets.
* `/Textures`: Custom-made textures made with Affinity used within the assets.
* `BouncingBall.blend`: The main Blender project file containing the timeline data, keyframes, and geometry.
